AI AGENT CHALLENGE SANDBOX PROBLEM
On this page, you can train in sandbox mode and experience the challenge mechanics before challenge day.

Important Notes:

Credits: No credits are provided in sandbox mode. You can create an OpenRouter account and use the free LLMs available for practice. On challenge day, you will receive credits as stated in the How It Works section.

Datasets: The sandbox provides the first three datasets for practice. On challenge day, there will be five datasets total—datasets 4-5 will unlock based on the competition rules.

How to Practice:

Download the problem statement for the training datasets below
Develop and test your solution
Submit your training solutions as many times as you want to see your score
Once satisfied with your approach, download the corresponding evaluation dataset
Run your solution on the evaluation dataset to get your final score
Remember: You can upload your evaluation dataset solution only once per dataset!

Get Ready: New to AI Agents? Start with our learning path in the Learn & Train section to understand the fundamentals of agent creation. Then review the How It Works section for complete challenge details and rules.
